## Introduction

## Installation
1. Download the latest version of [Fiji](https://fiji.sc/). 
2. Enable the FRET Imaging [update site](https://imagej.net/update-sites/following).
3. Once installed, go to _Plugins > FRET Imaging > AccPbFRET_.
